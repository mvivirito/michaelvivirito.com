About Me

I am a passionate Systems Administrator with experience in the AWS cloud and on-prem datacenters. I have been using computers since I was 3 years old and have matured alongside the technology.

I am mostly self-taught but began my foray into a formal education in 2017. Since then I have achieved an Associate degree in CS, Math and soon Physics.

My favorite OS is FreeBSD, and I am an avid Vim Golfer. In my spare time I am working on several personal projects on AWS. They are fully Serverless and implementing technologies such as S3, CloudFront, Route53, Cognito, API Gateway, Lambda and DynamoDB. I use Github, CodeDeploy with CodePipeline for continuous integration and deployment.
